Who are we?


Care For You has been rescuing and caring for wildlife for almost 35 years and is the largest wildlife rescue organisation in Australia. Care For You has over 3,000 volunteers in 28 NSW branches involved in the rescue and care of wildlife, and we have a dedicated Rescue Office that operates 24/7, 365 days a year assisting the community to help native animals in distress.

Why Choose Us?


We understand that you are passionate about animals, but may not have the time or capacity to help. Care For You utilises your donations in order to keep animals safe and live a fulfilling life.

100% Transparency

Care For You is a Not-For-Profit establishment, meaning 100% of your donations go straight to animals in need.

Personalised, Unique Connections

You will recieve email or mail updates about your chosen virtually adopted animal as a thanks!
